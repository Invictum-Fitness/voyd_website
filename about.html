The VOYD Fitness Story

Stop paying for
your own progress.

VOYD Fitness was built out of frustration. We believe that the tools to track your workouts and nutrition should be accessible to everyone, not hidden behind a subscription.

Vision

One platform. Zero friction.

Why switch between three different apps to track your bench press, your calories, and your weight? VOYD Fitness was built to centralize your evolution.

By bringing workouts and nutrition together under one sleek interface, you get a bird's-eye view of your progress that separate apps simply can't provide. Everything you need to improve yourself, synced in one place.

The end of the "Pay-to-click" era

We've all been there: you download an app, get excited to start, and hit a paywall the moment you try to add a second workout or check your macros. It's frustrating and demotivating.

At VOYD Fitness, the essentials are free. Period. No limits on your logs, no locked basic exercises, and no constant pop-ups. We only offer premium features for those who want to take it to the absolute limit—never to stop you from starting.
