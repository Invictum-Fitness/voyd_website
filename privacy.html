Privacy Policy

Transparent data handling and medical disclaimers for the VOYD Fitness community.

Last Updated: May 3, 2026

Introduction

At VOYD Fitness, privacy is our core value. We collect only what is necessary to provide you with an elite fitness experience. This policy outlines how we handle your data and our commitment to your safety.

Data Collection

We collect information essential for the functionality of VOYD Fitness:

  • Account Info: Your email address for syncing and account security.
  • Physical Characteristics: Data such as height, weight, gender, and age, which you provide to calculate your nutritional goals (calories, macronutrients).
  • App Content: Your workouts and progress logs are encrypted and stored securely.
  • Technical Data: Basic device info to ensure app stability.
  • Purchases: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat receives anonymous transaction data to verify your premium status. No credit card details are stored on our servers; these are handled exclusively by Apple or Google.

AI Processing (VOYD Fitness Coach)

Our VOYD Fitness AI Coach is powered by the Gemini API. To ensure your privacy:

  • No Personal Identifiers: We do not send your email address, password, or real name to the AI provider.
  • Enterprise Grade Privacy: We use the paid, professional tier of the Gemini API. This means your chat data is not used by the provider to train their global AI models.
  • Contextual Data: Only fitness-related data you provide in the chat is processed to give you accurate coaching.

Data Deletion & Rights

You have the right to access or delete your data at any time. We comply with GDPR and global privacy standards.

Account Deletion

To permanently delete your VOYD Fitness account and all associated health data, please email:

support@voydfitness.com

Data will be fully purged from our systems within 30 days of your request.

Contact Us

Questions regarding your privacy? Contact us at support@voydfitness.com.
