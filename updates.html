What's New in VOYD

Every update, every fix, every feature — documented clearly so you always know what changed and why.

1.0.8
v1.0.8 Enhancement 12 March 2026

Performance & Social Polish

We've refined the social experience and optimized how you interact with your data. This update focuses on speed and a more consistent user profile.

  • Workout Summaries: Get a detailed breakdown of your performance immediately after finishing a session.
  • Faster Search: Re-engineered the product search algorithm for near-instant results.
  • Competition Logic: Fixed a bug that allowed multiple invites to be sent to the same user.
  • Navigation: Updated the navigation bar for better reachability and a cleaner look.
  • Profile Persistence: Resolved an issue where profile pictures would sometimes reset after an app update.
1.0.7
v1.0.7 Feature Update 25 February 2026

The Competitions Update

We've added competitions for all users. You can now challenge your friends in a battle!

  • Competitions: Added competitions for lifting available for all users.
  • Template Startup: We fixed templates not opening in a new workout when pressed on the home screen.
  • Text Overflow: We fixed workout names overflowing off of the screen.
🤖
v1.0.6 Platform Expansion 26 February 2026

VOYD x Android: The Horizon Expands

The wait is officially over. After navigating the rigorous testing phases of the Google Play Store, VOYD is finally available for the Android ecosystem. Engineered for performance, built for discipline.

  • Play Store Official: Fully verified and live for all modern Android devices.
  • Unified Experience: The same premium VOYD feel, now optimized for the Android framework.
  • Universal Sync: Seamlessly switch between iOS and Android with real-time cloud mirroring.
  • Performance First: Zero compromise on speed, ensuring the smoothest tracking experience yet.
1.0.6
v1.0.6 Feature Update 25 February 2026

The Premium Knowledge Update

We've unlocked a new level of training for our premium users. From AI-generated templates to deep-dive exercise instructions, training has never been this smart.

  • Exercise Library: Added high-quality videos and expert explanations for all exercises.
  • Premium Templates: Premium users can now generate, edit, and use advanced workout templates.
  • Weekly Planning: Templates now include weekdays for a structured training schedule.
  • Smart Home Screen: For premium users, the home screen now intelligently shows your *next* scheduled workout instead of the previous one.
  • Technical: Rebuilt PR exercise keys to ensure 100% data consistency during cloud syncing.
  • Quality of Life: Fixed an issue where the tutorial kept reappearing, and ensured workout names no longer default to the last exercise name.
1.0.5
v1.0.5 Hotfix 19 February 2026

Critical Stability Patch

A quick but vital update to address a startup crash on specific devices. We worked closely with the App Store for an expedited review.

  • Crash Fix: Resolved a major bug causing the app to crash on launch by delaying the secure storage initialization.
  • Optimized Startup: Refined the boot sequence for a faster and more reliable app entry.
1.0.4
v1.0.4 Enhancement 18 February 2026

Refined Training & Premium Foundation

This update bridges the gap between basic tracking and elite planning, introducing the foundation for our Premium tier.

  • Advanced Logging: Workout editing now includes precise control over date and duration.
  • Premium Preview: First setup of the Premium Card and Premium Template system (coming soon for all users).
  • iOS Security: Migrated to the `flutter_secure_storage` package for industry-leading data protection on Apple devices.
  • New Home Screen: Complete layout refresh of the home screen for better overview and faster navigation.
  • Hero Tags: Implemented Flutter Hero animations for smooth, cinematic transitions between screens.
1.0.3
v1.0.3 Enhancement 6 January 2026

The Training Flow Update

Small tweaks, big impact. We focused on making your workout session smoother and providing more context for every set you perform.

  • Smart Timers: Tapping the check icon now triggers your rest timer immediately — no more manual starting.
  • Performance History: Exercises now display your previous performance (reps x weight) in real-time as you train.
  • Full Flexibility: Both exercises and personal goals are now fully editable to match your evolving routine.
  • Direct Support: Added quick-access buttons that link directly to our new Help & Support center.

v1.0.2 Major Refinement 31 December 2025

The Experience Update

We're closing out the year with a massive overhaul of the user experience, focusing on visual clarity and data-driven insights.

v1.0.1 Bug Fix 19 December 2025

Stability & Logic Patch

Our first post-launch patch focuses on data integrity and squashing the initial bugs reported by our early adopters.

  • Data Logic: Fixed a calculation error where the app incorrectly recalled previous weight and repetition data.
  • Goal Syncing: Resolved a date-stamping issue that caused old goals to override current ones.
  • Polish: Corrected several typos and improved text clarity in the onboarding screens.
